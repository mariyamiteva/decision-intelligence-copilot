# Vendor Assessment Policy

The organization evaluates vendors across capability, security, scalability, cost, and implementation risk.

## Selection rules
- A vendor with mature integration capabilities and clear service ownership is generally preferred.
- Strong security posture and documented incident response are mandatory for production-critical use cases.
- Solutions that reduce manual work by at least 25 percent should receive positive weighting.
- If implementation depends heavily on custom maintenance, risk should be elevated.
- Where pricing is attractive but delivery risk is high, the case should be flagged for review instead of immediate approval.

## Risk indicators
- Missing SLA definition
- Unclear support model
- Weak audit trail
- High dependency on a single expert
