# Change Management Guidelines

## Delivery and adoption
- Any selected option should have a feasible rollout plan within one quarter unless the business case explicitly justifies a longer timeline.
- Teams should favor solutions with transparent monitoring, rollback procedures, and ownership boundaries.
- A proposal that improves productivity but creates new opaque failure modes should be treated cautiously.
- User adoption risk should be assessed through training effort, process change, and operational burden.

## Escalation guidance
- Cases with conflicting source evidence should be escalated for human review.
- Cases with missing compliance, security, or operational ownership information should not be approved automatically.
